# Gold Investment: The Timeless Safe Haven

#### Portfolio Diversification

## Forms of Gold Investment

### Physical Gold

#### Gold Jewellery
- **Traditional**: Traditional form of gold investment in India
- **Dual Purpose**: Investment + personal use
- **Making Charges**: Additional making charges
- **Purity**: Usually 22K or 18K purity
- **Liquidity**: Good liquidity but lower than other forms
- **Storage**: Requires secure storage

#### Gold Coins and Bars
- **Pure Gold**: Usually 24K pure gold
- **Standardized**: Standardized weights and purity
- **Making Charges**: Lower making charges than jewellery
- **Liquidity**: High liquidity
- **Storage**: Requires secure storage
- **Authentication**: Need to verify authenticity

#### Gold ETFs
- **Exchange Traded**: Traded on stock exchanges
- **Paper Gold**: Paper form of gold investment
- **Liquidity**: High liquidity
- **Storage**: No storage required
- **Costs**: Lower costs than physical gold
- **Transparency**: Transparent pricing

#### Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGBs)
- **Government Issued**: Issued by Government of India
- **Interest**: 2.5% annual interest
- **Tax Benefits**: No capital gains tax on maturity
- **Liquidity**: Can be traded on exchanges
- **Safety**: Government guarantee
- **Maturity**: 8-year maturity with exit option after 5 years

### Digital Gold

#### Digital Gold Platforms
- **Online Purchase**: Buy gold online through platforms
- **Small Amounts**: Can buy small amounts
- **Storage**: Secure storage by platform
- **Liquidity**: Easy to sell online
- **Convenience**: Convenient online purchase
- **Costs**: Lower costs than physical gold

#### Gold Accumulation Plans
- **Regular Investment**: Invest fixed amount regularly
- **Systematic**: Systematic gold accumulation
- **Flexibility**: Flexible investment amounts

## Taxation of Gold Investments

### Physical Gold
- **Capital Gains Tax**: 20% tax on long-term capital gains
- **Short-Term Gains**: Taxed at income tax slabs
- **Wealth Tax**: No wealth tax on gold
- **GST**: GST on making charges
- **TDS**: No TDS on gold investments

### Gold ETFs
- **Capital Gains Tax**: 20% tax on long-term capital gains
- **Short-Term Gains**: Taxed at income tax slabs
- **Dividend Distribution**: No dividend distribution tax
- **Securities Transaction Tax**: STT on transactions
- **Wealth Tax**: No wealth tax

### Sovereign Gold Bonds
- **Interest Income**: 2.5% interest taxable at income tax slabs
- **Capital Gains**: No capital gains tax on maturity
- **Early Redemption**: Tax implications for early redemption
- **Wealth Tax**: No wealth tax
- **Tax Benefits**: Tax benefits on maturity
